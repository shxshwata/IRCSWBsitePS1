First Aid Training Program

We organise first-aid training regularly to help citizens of the state to learn the basic first-aid skills. The training is conducted by the staff of the Indian Red Cross West Bengal State Branch.

Blood Donation Camps

We organise blood donation camps frequently and coordinate with Central Blood Bank Authority. The benefits of donating blood include helping people injured in accidents, undergoing cancer treatment, and battling blood diseases, among other reasons.

SMS Campaign during COVID-19

SMS stands for S-Soaps/Sanitizers, M-Mask, S-Social Distancing. This campaign was done to minimise the spread of the COVID-19 virus. Red Cross SHQ staff & volunteers distributed masks, soaps & sanitary napkins to people in multiple district and sub-division branches.

No curable medicines were found for COVID-19 Pandemic. So we stressed on preventive measures. There was an awareness campaign carried out about wearing masks when outside, washing hands with soaps frequently and to maintain physical distance to prevent the spread of corona infections from time to time. More than 1,50,000 masks were distrubuted during the campaigns in the biggest festival of Bengal to generate interests. The main theme of the programme was "Prevention is better than cure."

Relief Programmes

The cyclone AMPHAN devastated coastal districts of South Bengal in 2020. The cyclone YAAS affected badly the lives of many people in 2021. Often, large areas of South Bengal and parts of North Bengal are flooded due to incessant rains and release of water from dams.cRed Cross like all other occasions of disasters in the past tries its best to reach the needy and affected people with relief materials like tarpoulins, hygiene kits, mosquito nets, clothes, food packets, buckets, etc.
